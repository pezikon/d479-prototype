Adventure Awaits

Beyond its stunning natural beauty, Taniti offers a vibrant array of entertainment options to enrich your visit. While many are drawn to the island's exquisite beaches, lush rainforests, and the active volcano, there's so much more to discover. Immerse yourself in local history at the museum, embark on exciting chartered fishing tours, or explore the underwater world with snorkeling. For thrill-seekers, zip-lining through the rainforest provides an unforgettable adventure. Enjoy the evening at various pubs, including a microbrewery, or dance the night away at the new dance club. You can also catch a movie, take breathtaking helicopter rides, have fun at an arcade, browse art galleries, or enjoy a game of bowling. Many of these exciting activities are conveniently located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. And for golf enthusiasts, a nine-hole golf course will be ready for play next year.

Unforgettable Experiences

Enjoy the Beaches

Relax on the soft, white sands or explore the unique rocky shores, perfect for sunbathing, swimming, or simply soaking in the island's beauty.

Explore the Rainforest

Immerse yourself in the lush, vibrant ecosystem of Taniti's tropical rainforests, offering a refreshing escape into nature.

Visit the Volcano

Take an exhilarating trip to Taniti's small but active volcano, a must-see natural wonder.

Discover Local History

Step back in time at the local history museum and learn about Taniti's rich indigenous heritage.

Go on Chartered Fishing Tours

Cast your line into the abundant waters surrounding Taniti for an exciting deep-sea fishing adventure.

Snorkel in Clear Waters

Dive into the crystal-clear waters and witness the vibrant marine life thriving beneath the surface.

Experience Zip-lining

Soar through the rainforest canopy on an exhilarating zip-line, offering breathtaking views and an adrenaline rush.

Dance at the New Dance Club

Groove to the rhythm and let loose at Taniti's new dance club, perfect for a night out.

See a Movie

Catch the latest blockbuster or a timeless classic at Taniti's local cinema.

Take a Helicopter Ride

Get a bird's-eye view of the stunning island landscape with an unforgettable helicopter tour.

Play at an Arcade

Challenge your friends and family to a variety of games at the lively arcade.

Visit Art Galleries

Admire local and international artwork at Taniti's captivating art galleries.

Go Bowling

Enjoy a fun-filled evening of friendly competition at the bowling alley.

Visit Local Pubs

Unwind and enjoy the local atmosphere at one of Taniti's charming pubs, including a unique microbrewery.

Play Golf (Coming Soon)

Perfect your swing at the new nine-hole golf course, set to be operational by next year.
