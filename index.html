Discover the Heart of the Pacific

From bustling food markets to white sandy beaches, Taniti is an island of beautiful contrasts.

Beach Bliss & Ocean Adventures

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Taniti's diverse coastlines. The serene sandy shores are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, or simply enjoying the ocean breeze.

Rainforest Exploration & Ziplining Thrills

Embark on an unforgettable journey through lush tropical rainforests, and for the adventurous, experience the exhilaration of zip-lining through the vibrant canopy.

Volcano Vista & Island Tours

Discover the majestic beauty of Taniti's active volcano with guided tours, or explore the island's charm and native architecture on scenic boat or bus excursions.

Cultural Immersion & Local Delights

Delve into Tanitian culture by visiting the local history museum and savoring the authentic flavors of the island at restaurants serving fresh local fish and rice.

Unforgettable Experiences

Underwater Wonders & Fishing Expeditions

Dive into the crystal-clear waters for an incredible snorkeling experience, or try your hand at deep-sea fishing with chartered tours.

Merriton Landing Nightlife & Entertainment

As the sun sets, head to Merriton Landing, a lively hub offering a variety of entertainment options including pubs, a microbrewery, a new dance club, and an arcade.

Plan Your Perfect Getaway

Where to Stay

from an inexpensive hostel to a four-star resort, find the perfect place to call your island home.

Getting Around

Explore the island by rental car, bus, taxi, or mountain bike!

When to Visit

Enjoy warm, tropical weather year-round. The dry season from May to October offers endless sun-drenched days.
